A Ministry of Service

Monrovia Christian Fellowship has served the people of Monrovia for generations, opening its doors to all who seek rest, guidance, and belonging. Under the leadership of Pastor Momolu Dukuly, our fellowship is built on prayer, community outreach, and a shared commitment to lifting one another up in faith.

"Come all tired and weary souls;
You shall find your rest here."
